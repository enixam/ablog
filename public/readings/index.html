Preface

This project is highly motivated and inspired by stats337 at Stanford University offered by Hadley Wickham, and Data Science with R: A Resource Compendium by Martin Monkman. They both provided great reading materials in data analysis with R, or applied data science in general. Here I attempt to finish one or two papers per week, draw a brief summary, and document my personal thoughts.
